    • Master Mobile UX: NOTES

    • Design for the smallest, least powerful device first. That will help in focusing on content and core functionality
    • We have to rewire our brains to adapt to new technologies.
    • DO MOBILE FIRST. Responsive is NOT mobile first
    • Know your users better than they know themselves. Don't listen to what they say, observe what they do!
    • Be there, be useful & be quick!
    • Users want: reachability, speed (images, css & js, gzip)
    • Primary controls at the bottom - Reachability
    • Perform research to understand user's perceptions
    • Make forms shorter and the fields larger. Only ask for what is truly needed. Paginate form if is large
    • Avoid pop-ups. Make adds small and simple
    • Users don't visit websites to look for ads
